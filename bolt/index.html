Bolt

Bolt is a project management app for freelancers and agencies/studios with multiple products ~ Invoicing being the first feature.

Goals

Atticus’ team was rolling out a new marketing site and an app that’s centered around a bunch of form elements to help users evaluate their cases and get advice on what they could pursue next.

  1. Simple invoicing - Smart invoices and integrated payments
  2. Design Layout for Invoice A4
  3. Recurring Invoices
  4. Payments via Paypal + ACH Bank Wire Transfers
  5. Customise invoices ~ Initially 2 Invoice Types: Simple and Modern
  6. URL based invoices + Invoices sent on mails
  7. Export in PDF format

Invoices

User details - + Number, + Full Name, + Description [ 180 words ] , + Issue Date, + Notes ( Optional), + Currency (default USD), + Due Date, +Company Name/Client Name

Flow

User signs up to bolt > Clicks on create a new Invoice > Fills out details > Clicks Create Invoice or Preview Invoice > Gets Redirected to a new url

My Role

I designed the mobile flow of the app and then worked on the UI/UX of the app and designed the mobile marketing site.

The Approach

We iterated the flow and wanted the mobile app to be as simple as possible eventually bringing a more delightful experience to the boring process of Invoicing.
