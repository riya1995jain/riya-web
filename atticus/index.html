Atticus

Atticus helps people tackle serious legal issues of all stripes. Get expert advice on your options, referrals to great lawyers, and hands-on support as your case moves.

Goals

Atticus’ team was rolling out a new marketing site and an app that’s centered around a bunch of form elements to help users evaluate their cases and get advice on what they could pursue next.

There are about 12 different flows for different kinds of cases like disability, immigration, accidents etc. Consistency is one thing but we needed to figure out a way to make the app as clear as possible and provide guideance as and when required by the users.

The Approach

To guide users we made sure we had a virtual assistant guiding them prompting hints as and when necessary and to make the form conversational rather than a boring form.

Designing the Elements

The Atticus’ team was already setup in Figma and we made sure everything was componentized to make sure scaling from here would be easy.
